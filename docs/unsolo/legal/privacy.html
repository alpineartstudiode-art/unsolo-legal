Privacy Policy (English)

This privacy policy is applicable to the UnSolo app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by AlpineArtStudio (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. AlpineArtStudio is the Data Controller for your personal data. This service is provided "AS IS".

1. What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application collects information you provide during onboarding and while using the app. You may use UnSolo as a guest after completing onboarding, or optionally link an Apple or Google account to save your profile across devices.

Depending on how you use the Application, you may provide:

  • Account information: Display name and profile photo. If you link Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In, we also receive your email address and account identifier from that provider.
  • User Content: Preferred languages, sporting activities (equipment), skill levels, travel destinations (locations), travel dates, personal bio, and trip goals.
  • Messages and interactions: Chat messages and matching activity with other users.
  • Optional payments: If you choose to send an optional tip, payment is processed entirely by Apple or Google; we do not receive or store your payment card details.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application and our service providers may automatically process certain technical information necessary to operate the service, including device type, operating system, app version, push notification tokens, and network connectivity status. If you opt in to partner offers on the Offers tab, Google AdMob may process advertising identifiers and related device data for ad delivery and measurement. Our backend providers (such as Supabase) may process IP addresses and request metadata for security and service delivery. After you accept the in-app terms and privacy consent, we use Mixpanel to process product analytics events (for example screens viewed and key in-app interactions) to improve the Application, and Firebase Crashlytics (Google) to process crash and diagnostic logs to maintain stability. These analytics and crash tools are not enabled before that consent. We do not use Firebase Analytics for product event tracking in the current release.

Offline image cache on your device

To improve offline use, the Application may store profile photos you have already viewed on your device (for example, chat partner avatars prefetched from the Chats tab). This cache is kept in the app's private storage area, not in your photo gallery. It is used only to display images when you have no internet connection.

When you log out, we clear this image cache on the device along with other local session data. Cached images are not shared with other users.

2. Location information

UnSolo does not continuously track your real-time GPS location in the background. When you search for or select a trip destination, we process the place information you choose (such as place name, address, and coordinates) using the Google Places API. Other users see the destination you set in your profile for matching purposes. You may optionally open a selected place in your device's maps app via an external link.

Yes. We share information with trusted third-party service providers that help us run the Application. Their use of your data is governed by their respective privacy policies:

Для зручності офлайн-використання Додаток може зберігати на пристрої фото профілів, які ви вже переглядали (наприклад, аватари співрозмовників із вкладки «Чати»). Під час виходу з облікового запису ми очищаємо цей кеш зображень на пристрої разом з іншими локальними даними сесії. Кешовані зображення не передаються іншим користувачам.
