Community Guidelines (English)

These Community Guidelines explain what is allowed on UnSolo and how we handle reports. They supplement our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. UnSolo is an 18+ app for adults seeking travel and activity partners.

Last updated: 2026-07-11

1. Public profile photos (Discover)

Your main profile photo is visible to others before you match. It must be appropriate for a public 18+ dating context.

  • Allowed: Travel, sport, social portraits, non-explicit swimwear within reasonable community standards.
  • Not allowed: Nudity, sexual acts, pornographic staging, or any content involving minors in a sexual context (zero tolerance).

2. Prohibited content and behavior

  • Harassment, bullying, stalking, or hate speech
  • Threats or encouragement of violence
  • Spam, scams, or unauthorized commercial solicitation
  • Impersonation or misrepresentation
  • Sexual content or nudity in public profile areas
  • Any content involving minors in a sexual context
  • Illegal activity

3. How to report

You can report a user from chat, Discover, or their profile. Choose the category that best describes the issue:

  • Harassment or bullying
  • Hate speech
  • Sexual content or nudity
  • Spam or scam
  • Impersonation
  • Underage concern
  • Threats or violence
  • Something else (optional details)

When you submit a report, the user is blocked and you will not see them again. You may also contact us via Settings → Support.

4. What happens after you report

  • We log the report and review it according to severity.
  • We do not tell the reported user who submitted the report.
  • We may remove content, warn, restrict, suspend, or permanently ban accounts.
  • Critical categories (underage concerns, threats) are prioritized for review.

5. Contact

Safety and support: unsolo.box@gmail.com
