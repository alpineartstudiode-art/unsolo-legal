1. What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application collects information you provide during onboarding and while using the app. You may use SnowBuddy as a guest after completing onboarding, or optionally link an Apple or Google account to save your profile across devices.

Depending on how you use the Application, you may provide:

  • Account information: Display name and profile photo. If you link Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In, we also receive your email address and account identifier from that provider.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application and our service providers may automatically process certain technical information necessary to operate the service, including device type, operating system, app version, push notification tokens, and network connectivity status. Our backend providers (such as Supabase) may process IP addresses and request metadata for security and service delivery. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs (such as Firebase Analytics or Mixpanel) in the current release.

2. Location information

SnowBuddy does not continuously track your real-time GPS location in the background. When you search for or select a trip destination, we process the place information you choose (such as place name, address, and coordinates) using the Google Places API. Other users see the destination you set in your profile for matching purposes. You may optionally open a selected place in your device's maps app via an external link.
